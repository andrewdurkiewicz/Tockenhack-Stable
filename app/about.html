About SeedScholar

SeedScholar is a platform that allows students from around the world to seek funding for their educations from semi-anonymous benefactors, with the university acting as mediator. SeedScholar takes a new approach to scholarships by creating a one-on-one contract between student and sponsor in which the student gets paid out in funding rounds according to their academic performance. The platform incentivizes students to study more in order to receive full funding, while the sponsor knows that they will get back whatever money that the student doesn't earn.

By using an Ethereum smart contract designed by us, we aim to disrupt the world of sponsorships. Now it is possible for a person in the United States to sponsor any student from places as various as Ethiopia to Argentina with zero friction and one hundred percent confidence to both parties. Universities and schools must opt-in to the network to act as mediators, and thereafter students can apply for sponsorship funds on the platform. Schools routinely report in on the grades of their students and the funding is released proportional to the student's performance with all of the remaining funds getting returned to the sponsor. Anyone in the world can browse the lists of students and choose to sponsor an individual's education based on their background profile or field of study. Additionally, it lowers the barrier for people to become scholarship sponsors and allows sponsors to stake as little as $1000 dollars toward someone's education. We believe that SeedScholar is an innovative approach to using smart contracts for frictionless scholarships.
